Project Overview

We have the task of working with the Ole School Alumni Group in helping create opportunities for students in the Ole School group with potential opportunities for employment and internships and helping pave the way for the future. We will create a searchable database that allows students, alumni, sponsors, and mentors to load resumes, job postings, and opportunities within the Ole School group.

Project Goals

• Develop the best functioning system we can in the desired time frame
• Design a system that helps Ole School Alumni Group reach their goals more effectively

Audience

The Ole School Alumni Group prides itself on being a Visionary, Leader, Advocate, and Catalyst. Our audience for the project it the members, mentors, students and potential companies that are associated with the Ole School Alumni Group.

Project Management Plan:

Our team has currently been using iMessage/Texting for the means of most of our communication. We currently are using a Microsoft Teams Page as a home storage place for our documentation as we start drafts and work to create our completed project. Within the Teams page, we will organize our files in folders by each sprint along with additional folders for things such as client documentation, professor files, etc. As we start to coordinate our class schedules together, we plan to start meeting in-person following break. Right now, we have been meeting within Teams as it allows easy integration between meetings and our files. Additionally, we have a Trello tab setup on our page. This will allow us to easily organize our product backlog with each sprint backlog and allow our team to have transparency on all tasks throughout each sprint.
